Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How does the video call shopping work?

A: After adding items to your cart and proceeding to order, you can initiate a WhatsApp chat with our representative. They will show you the available produce live, and you can select your preferred items and grades.

Q: Are prices really negotiable?

A: Yes! Our prices are not fixed. You can negotiate with our team during the WhatsApp interaction based on the daily market rates and the quantity you wish to purchase.

Q: Is there a platform fee?

A: No, Z-Mart charges zero platform fees or commissions. You only pay for the produce and a transparent delivery charge.

Q: How quickly will my order be delivered?

A: We strive for fast delivery after your order is confirmed. Delivery times depend on your location in Chennai and market conditions, but we aim for the quickest possible service.
